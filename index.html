A clinical pharmacy that practices with efficiency, care and accuracy

Seamless Care is one of the largest clinical pharmacies serving the GTA. With a number of highly-focused pharmacists on the team, our goal is to provide clinically appropriate, safe, and cost-effective therapy to agencies that want to meet their patients' needs. The partnerships we develop are beneficial to the individuals supported, their caregivers and families as well as to their existing healthcare providers.

Certificate & Training

We offer online training and certification programs for caregivers,case workers and nurses to enhance their skill set based on their own time and pace. Our programs are offered under two categories: role-based certifications and specialized certifications. Trainees can benefit from a tiered approach and build their expertise based on immediate and future needs.
